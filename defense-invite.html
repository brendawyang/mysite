What's a 'defense'?

In my department, we write a dissertation as a part of completing a PhD. If the process of getting a doctorate is learning to "farm" (to plow, sow, and harvest research), then our dissertation is the meal made from what we've grown. We also select a committee, which is the panel of judges/coaches who ultimately decide that plate of food meets the bar for a doctorate.

In this metaphor, the dissertation defense is when I actually get to share that meal, and let people know some of what I've been up to 6 years. ðŸ˜… More practically, it's an hour-ish talk where the student verbally presents the highlights of their dissertation. Our committee members come, and we can also invite other people, too!
